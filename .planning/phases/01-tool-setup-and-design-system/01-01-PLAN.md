  | Widget Size | CSS Behavior | Effective Width |

  - At lg with sidebar expanded: each StatCard ~178px wide (very constrained)
- Audit rule: "Any typography/padding recommendation that makes a half widget unreadable at ~360px wide must be rejected or qualified with a grid-impact note."
